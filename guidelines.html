Competition Guidelines

  • Users who participate in all 8 rounds can win up to 10 ETH
  • Selections can be submitted only once and cannot be corrected once submitted
  • In each round the winner must predict the top 5 cryptocurencies that will have the greatest 7 day price increase. The choices must be placed in decending order from the greatest increase to the least.
  • If more than one person guesses correctly, only the first submitted answer wins.
  • After 8 weeks, the person with the highest accumulated score (based on our point system) will win 2 ETH. In the event of a tie, the prize (2 ETH) will be divided equally among those tied.
  • In the event of a tie, the prize (2 ETH) will be divided equally among those tied.
  • Point system: 1 point is awarded for each correct currency guessed. 3 points are awarded for each correct currency in its proper place (1-5).
  • During the 8 week period ChainBits may add bonus question for extra points.
  • In order to be eligible for the weekly/highest score prize you must 1. follow @chainbitsweb on twitter 2. retweet the pinned message.
Results are based on coinmarketmetrics.com from a list of cryptocurrencies with a market cap greater than or equal to 50M.
